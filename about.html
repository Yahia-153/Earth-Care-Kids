About Earth Care Kids

Earth-Care-Kids is a program designed to educate children about environmental conservation. It focuses on teaching kids how to recycle, conserve energy, and reduce the use of fuels that emit harmful gases. The program includes exciting games that make learning about the environment fun and engaging, encouraging kids to become recycling superheroes while having a blast.

Our Story

We are a team that was formed in 2024 to create this website with the aim of helping the environment. We are young people interested in cleaning the environment and preserving it.
